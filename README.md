## 🚨 Is Your PC Slow Even When CPU Usage Is Low?
Windows 11 may automatically enable **Efficiency Mode (EcoQoS)**, lowering process scheduling priority — even when your system isn't under heavy load. This often leads to the notorious **"Green Leaf" icon** in Task Manager, throttling your most important apps.

### This can cause:
- **Docker & WSL2 (vmmem) Sluggishness:** One of the primary reasons why Docker runs slow on Windows 11.
- **Sluggish UI & Input Lag:** Delayed response in professional software and IDEs.
- **Background App Throttling:** Compilations, rendering tasks, and scripts taking longer than necessary.

---

## 💡 What EffOff Does
EffOff is a lightweight background utility that acts as a **performance watchdog**:
✔ **Monitors all processes** in real-time.
✔ **Automatically disables** unwanted Efficiency Mode/EcoQoS.
✔ **Fixes Docker & WSL2 throttling** issues instantly.
✔ **Starts with Windows** and requires no manual intervention.
✔ **Minimal system resources** (Under 0.1% CPU usage).

## 🔬 Why This Matters
Efficiency Mode reduces CPU scheduling priority. Even when CPU usage is low (e.g., 5%), Windows may still throttle certain processes like **Docker's vmmem**. This creates the illusion of "low usage but slow performance." EffOff restores full scheduling priority automatically.
